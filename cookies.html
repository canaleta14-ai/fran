Cookie Policy

Last Updated: December 11, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

Cookies help us understand how you use our website, remember your preferences, and improve your overall experience on Love Hibo.

2. How We Use Cookies

Love Hibo uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Operation: To enable core website functionality
  • Performance: To analyze how visitors use our website
  • Functionality: To remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics: To gather statistics about website traffic
  • Affiliate Tracking: To track Amazon affiliate links and commissions

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

Examples:

  • Session cookies
  • Security cookies
  • Service Worker registration

3.2 Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are visited most often. This data is aggregated and anonymous, helping us improve website performance.

Examples:

  • Google Analytics (if implemented)
  • Page load time tracking
  • Error reporting

3.3 Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as language preference or region) and provide enhanced, personalized features.

Examples:

  • Language selection
  • Filter preferences
  • Newsletter subscription status
  • PWA installation preferences

3.4 Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages:

Third Parties:

  • Amazon: Affiliate tracking cookies when you click product links
  • Google Fonts: Font loading and caching
  • Font Awesome CDN: Icon delivery
  • Social Media Platforms: If social sharing buttons are used

4. Cookie Duration

4.1 Session Cookies

Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. These help with navigation and security during your visit.

4.2 Persistent Cookies

Cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. These remember your preferences across multiple visits.

Typical Duration:

  • Service Worker cache: Until manually cleared or updated
  • Amazon affiliate cookies: Up to 24 hours (set by Amazon)
  • Analytics cookies: Typically 2 years
  • Preference cookies: 1 year

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

5.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. You can set your browser to:

  • Block all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies only
  • Delete cookies when you close your browser
  • Be notified when cookies are set

5.2 How to Delete Cookies

Instructions for popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Clear browsing data → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Clear Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data → Remove All
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Clear browsing data → Cookies

⚠️ Important: Blocking or deleting cookies may affect your experience on our website. Some features may not function properly without cookies enabled.

6. Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that signals to websites that you don't want to have your online activity tracked. Our website respects DNT signals where technically feasible.

7. Amazon Cookies

When you click on Amazon product links on our website, Amazon may set cookies on your device to:

  • Track affiliate referrals for commission purposes
  • Remember items in your Amazon cart
  • Provide personalized recommendations
  • Enable secure transactions

These cookies are governed by Amazon's privacy policy and cookie policy, not by Love Hibo. Please review Amazon's Cookie Notice for more information.

8. Progressive Web App (PWA) Data

If you install Love Hibo as a Progressive Web App (PWA), additional data may be stored locally on your device to enable offline functionality:

  • Service Worker Cache: Stores website files for offline access
  • App Manifest: Contains app configuration and icons
  • Local Storage: May store user preferences and settings

You can clear this data by uninstalling the PWA or clearing your browser cache.

By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this Cookie Policy. If you do not agree with our use of cookies, you should adjust your browser settings accordingly or refrain from using our website.
